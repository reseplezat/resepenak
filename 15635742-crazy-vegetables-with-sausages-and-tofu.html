Crazy Vegetables with Sausages and Tofu

Dipos pada March 2, 2022

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Crazy Vegetables with Sausages and Tofu:

  1. 4 eggs(4 butir telur)
  2. Garlic(bawang putih) 10 siung_chopped(cincang)
  3. Shallot(bawang merah) 8 siung_chopped(cincang)
  4. Leek(daun bawang) 5 helai_slice(iris tipis)
  5. 3 Cayenne peppers (cabai rawit)
  6. 5-6 of big red chili(cabai merah besar)
  7. Cabbege(kubis) 1/2 buah_slice(iris tipis)
  8. Caisim(sawi hijau)_1/2 rotion(stngah bonggol)
  9. Fredh Chicory(sawi putih segar)_1 rotion(1 bonggol)
  10. Salt(garam)_1 tsp(1 sendok teh)
  11. Powdered pepper(lada bubuk)_a few
  12. Soy sauce(kecap)_2 tbsp(2 sendok makan)
  13. Powdered mushroom broth(kaldu jamur bubuk)_a few
  14. Fiber cream_a few
  15. Palm oil_a few {You can use any oil that you have or margarine}

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Crazy Vegetables with Sausages and Tofu

1
Panaskan wajan beberapa menit. Pakai api sedang. Tumis bawang merah, bawang putih sampai harum. Lalu masukkan telur ke dalamnya. Tumis kembali sampai agak kering. Masukkan daun bawang, aduk hingga merata. Heat frying pan a few minutes. Moderate flame. Saute Onions, garlic till it smells good. Then, put some eggs in it. Saute back till it's rather dry. Put the leeks in, stir them all around.
2
Lalu masukkan sosis, aduk sebentar. Lalu masukkan tofu, aduk sampai merata. Tambahkan sedikit fiber cream(tidak perlu dicairkan). Then add some sausage, stir it for a while. Then, it is packed with tofu. Add a little fiber cream(no need to cash in).
3
Tambahkan seasoning(garam, lada bubuk, kaldu jamur bubuk), aduk hingga merata. Add seasoning(salt, powdered pepper, powdered mushroom broth), stirring until evenly.
4
Masukkan sayur satu per satu. Kubis, lalu aduk merata. Masukkan sawi putih segar, aduk merata kembali. Tambahkan kecap, aduk sampai merata. Add vegetables one by one. Cabbages, stir them all around. Put the fresh cabbage in, stir it evenly again. Add ketchup or soy sauce, stir it evenly.
5
Jika sudah tercampur rata, cek rasa sebelum disajikan. Lebih baik pakai api sedang. If it's even mixed together, check taste first before being served. Better get a medium flame.
6
Jika rasa sudah pas, sajikan. When the flavor is right, serve. Happy trying moms..ü§ó
