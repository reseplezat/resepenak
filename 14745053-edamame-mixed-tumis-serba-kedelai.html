Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat EDAMAME MIXED (TUMIS SERBA KEDELAI):

  1. 100 grms Edamame
  2. 125 grms White Tofu
  3. 50 grms Soy bean sprouts
  4. 90 grms Lobster Sausage
  5. 1 pc Egg
  6. 12 grms Garlics
  7. 30 grms Shallots
  8. 3 grms Ebi
  9. 3 grms Ginger
  10. 7.5 grms Camomile

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat EDAMAME MIXED (TUMIS SERBA KEDELAI)

1
Peel and clean washing Edamame.
2
Prepare soy bean sprouts. Clean up from Allah the impurities, washing clean and keep a side.
3
Clean washing and cutting tofu into smaller size. Use your own cutting style.
4
Clean up and peeling all the seasoning. Slice all into thinners layers. Ebi and Red Hot Chili
5
Also Garlic and SHALLOTS
6
Bit and break Ginger and Camomile
7
Prepare your pan. Make it hot and add some margarin, let it be melted.
8
Saute for a while start from Garlic, Ebi, SHALLOTS, Red Hot Chili, Ginger, and Lemon Grass
9
Add a little bit of salt, salty soy bean ketchup, sesame oil, and fish oil. Mixed well.
10
Add lobster sausage, tofu, edamame and stir well
11
Test you food. Add something do you need.
12
Take of the pan from stove. Add alm the soy bean sprout. And stir well.
13
Presentation. Put and share into thre plates. Toping: sesame seed and soy bean powder, a slice tomato, also garlic frief
