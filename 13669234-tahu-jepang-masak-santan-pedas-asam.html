  2. 2 sdm margarine
  3. 3 vegetable mix
  4. 350 ml air
  5. 1 bungkus santan bubuk
  6. 1/4 sdm pasta asam
  7. 1/2 mangkok kcl tempe sdh goreng
  8. 1 batang terong ungu

  10. 2 lembar daun jeruk
  11. 1 lembar daun salam
  12. 1/2 sdt gulpas
  13. 1/2 sdm garam+kaldu jamur
