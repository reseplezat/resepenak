Anda bisa membuat üçù Pasta with Goya Champuru (Tumis Pare khas Jepang) memakai 11 jenis bahan dan 7 langkah pembuatan. Berikut ini cara dalam membuat hidangannya.

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat üçù Pasta with Goya Champuru (Tumis Pare khas Jepang):

  1. 200 gr pasta, I use penne
  2. 1 medium bitter melon/goya/pare
  3. 1 block tofu
  4. 2 large eggs
  5. 1/4 onion, mince
  6. 1 garlic, mince
  7. 2 tsp soy sauce
  8. Cooking oil
  9. Salt
  10. Pepper
  11. Dashi/powdered stock

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat üçù Pasta with Goya Champuru (Tumis Pare khas Jepang)

1
Slice bitter melon lengthwise, scoop out its gut. Slice it 1/2 cm thick. To reduce the bitter taste, sprinke with salt and wait for 5 minutes then rinse it. You can also blanched it in hot water for 1 minute.
2
Cook pasta till al dente, reserve the water.
3
Heat oil in pan, tear up tofu and cook it until a bit browning. Set aside.
4
Heat oil in pan, add onion and garlic, stir until golden. Add bitter melon and tofu. Add soy sauce and dashi, stir in medium to high heat. Adjust the taste with salt and pepper. Lower the heat.
5
In a bowl, beat the egg lightly.
6
Pour the egg on to the pan, when it become half cooked, add penne and stir altogether with the dish. Add starchy pasta water to make it a bit creamy.
7
Serve it immediately.
