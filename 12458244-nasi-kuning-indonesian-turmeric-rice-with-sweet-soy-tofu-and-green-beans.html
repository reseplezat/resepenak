Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Nasi Kuning (Indonesian Turmeric Rice) with Sweet Soy Tofu and Green Beans:

  1. Nasi Kuning (Turmeric Rice)
  2. 2 cups long grain white rice
  3. 1/2 cups water
  4. 1 1/2 cup coconut nilk
  5. 3 tsp turmeric powder
  6. 1 stalk lemon grass
  7. 3-5 bay leaves
  8. 4 fresh kaffir lime leaves
  9. 1/3 shallot
  10. 3 minced garlic
  11. 1-2 tsp chicken powder / salt
  12. Fried shallot
  13. Tofu & Green Beans
  14. 300 g fried firm tofu
  15. 1 tbsp virgin olive oil
  16. 1 cup green bean
  17. 1/3 shallot
  18. 4 minced garlic
  19. 1 thai chilli
  20. 1/3 stalk lemon grass
  21. 2 bay leaves
  22. 3 tbsp sweet soy sauce
  23. 2 tbsp liquid amino
  24. 1/2 tsp chicken powder
  25. 1/3 bunch spring onion
  26. 1/2 tsp salt
  27. 5 shakes black pepper
  29. 1/3 lime

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Nasi Kuning (Indonesian Turmeric Rice) with Sweet Soy Tofu and Green Beans

1
Nasi Kuning
2
Rinse the rice until the water is clear. This is to make sure your rice won‚Äôt be starchy sticky. Add rice into rice cooker.
3

4

6

7

8

9
