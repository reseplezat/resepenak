Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat MIX YELLOW SOUP (SOTO, COTO, LESAH, LAKSA):

  1. 1/2 pak White Vermicelli
  2. 1 pc Tofu
  3. 1 pc Fish Roll
  4. 2 pcs Baso Meat Balls
  5. 1 pc Carrot
  6. 25 grams Grated Young Papaya
  7. 2 pcs Garlic
  8. 4 pcs Shallots
  9. 2 pcs Red Chile
  10. 2 pcs Green Chile
  11. 1 pc Red Jumbo Chile
  12. 1 pc Red Hot Chili Pepper
  13. 3 pcs Candlenuts
  14. 10 pcs Pepper seed
  15. 10 pcs Corriander seed
  16. 1/2 little finger of Ginger
  17. 1/2 little finger of Curcumin
  18. 1 Finger of Galangal Lengkuas
  19. 2 pcs Lime Leaves
  20. 2 pcs Bayleaves
  21. 2 pc Lemongrass
  22. 1 slice Cinnamon

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat MIX YELLOW SOUP (SOTO, COTO, LESAH, LAKSA)

1
Prepare all ingredient and seadoning. Wash, Peel and Cut in your own style
2
Grind all Pepper, Corriander and Cundlenuts to be a good pasta as well.
3
Bit and break the other seasoning such as garlic, shallots, Ginger, Galangal, Curcumin etc
4
Just slice all the chilies
5
Prepare your pan, took one spoon of margarin, let it be melted
6
Saute first the seasoning pasta until you get a good smelt. Then add the other ingredient. Mix them as well. Add around twoo liters water to care your seasoning. Add all Baso, Tofu, Carrot also fish roll. Let them for a while until the soup boiling. Keep some minute.
7
Add one egg and simmer it as well
8
Take out the pan from your stove. And finally add all the vermicelli, mix well.
9
Share into three or cups depend on
10
For toping you can add fried garlic, fried shallots, green sambal, red sambal, ketchup, slice of lemon, chili sausace and tomatoes sausace. Free!!!
