Oyster Sauce Tofu with Champignon

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Oyster Sauce Tofu with Champignon:

  1. (serving for 3-4 portion)
  2. 2 clove garlic, minced
  3. 3 clove red onion, minced
  4. 6 beans
  5. A cup of champignon mushroom, drained
  6. 2 packs of egg tofu
  7. 1/2 glass of water
  8. 5 tbs oyster sauce
  9. 3 tbs sweet soy sauce
  10. 4 tbs Worcester sauce
  11. 2 tsp cornstarch (maizena)
  12. 4 tbs olive oil
  13. salt and pepper

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Oyster Sauce Tofu with Champignon

1
Take out the tofu from its package, cut into two parts.
2
Preheat olive oil, then fry the tofu until it gets golden.
3
Mince garlic and red onion into small parts. Also cut beans about 5 cm long.
4
Boil beans in a pan for 15 minutes until the colour of the bean gets fresh.
5
Add minced red onion and garlic into the pan, 1/2 tsp of salt and pepper. Saute them until the onions are half cooked.
6
Add a cup of champignon (it has to be drained before), Add 5 tbs oyster sauce, 4 tbs Worcester sauce, 3 tbs sweet soy sauce. Stir them well.
7
Mix 2 tsp cornstarch with 1/2 glass of water, stir them well. Pour into the pan to thickened the liquid.
8
Add fried tofu and beans, stir it carefully, let them cooked perfectly.
