Spicy braised tofu (Dubu-jorim 두부조림)

It's standard in Korean dosirak (lunchbox) and totally vegetarian dish. It’s a tofu braised in a savory-spicy sauce and pan fried with caramelized onion, garlic, and green onions, making it juicy, a little spicy, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside. It’s a delicious side dish for rice, noodles or porridge, and so addictive 😋 Source @maangchi
