Bean Curd and Chicken soup with carrots

Categories : soup Cuisine : Chinese Ingredients : chicken (can be replaced with pork), bean curd

Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Bean Curd and Chicken soup with carrots:

  1. 3 dried bean curd (tofu skin) sheets
  2. 2 pcs carrots, peeled and sliced to bite size pieces
  3. 2 ginger, peeled
  4. 3 cloves garlic, crushed
  5. 150 gram chicken breast, chopped into 1 inch pieces
  6. 3 green onions, thinly sliced for garnish (green part only)
  7. Oyster sauce
  8. Light Soy sauce
  9. Sesame oil
  10. 3/4 tablespoon Salt
  11. 1/2 teaspoon Pepper
  12. 1/2 tbsp Sugar
  13. 1 liter water
  14. 2 teaspoons olive oil / vegetable oil

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Bean Curd and Chicken soup with carrots

1
Soak dried bean curd sheets in separate bowl with cold water to submerge them by one inch. Once rehydrated, tear the bean curd to 2 inch sections
2
Combined the chicken, half garlic and ginger in a bowl, seasoned with salt, pepper, oyster sauce and sesame oil. Marinated for 15 minutes.
3
Heat olive oil in a soup pot over medium-high heat and sautÃ©ed the garlic until fragrant. Place the marinated chicken and cook until lightly browned. Add the carrots.
4
Pour the water and let it boil for 10 minutes. Meanwhile drain the bean curd.
5
Add the bean curd and seasoned with soy sauce, oyster sauce, salt and sugar to taste. Bring to another boil.
6
Reduce heat and simmer to 20 minutes, or until all the ingredients are tender and fully cooked.
7
Turn off heat, garnish with onions and serve immediately.
