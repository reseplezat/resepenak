Bahan-bahan dan bumbu yang perlu dipersiapkan untuk membuat Dububuchim-yangnyeomjang aka Pan Fried Tofu with Spicy Sauces:

  1. 250 gr tofu
  2. 2 tsp vegetables oil
  3. seasoning sauce :
  4. 2 clove of minced garlic
  5. 2 chopped green onion
  6. 2 ts hot pepper flakes
  7. 2 ts honey
  8. 2 ts soy sauce
  9. 2 ts sesame oil
  10. some roasted sesame seeds for sprinkling

Langkah-langkah untuk membuat Dububuchim-yangnyeomjang aka Pan Fried Tofu with Spicy Sauces

1
Slice the tofu into 10 bite size pieces (¬º inch thick rectangles).¬†
2
Heat a pan and add 1 to 2 tbs of vegetable oil.
3
Add the tofu and lower the heat. Cook over low heat for about 5 to 7 minutes until the bottoms of the tofu pieces turn golden brown.
4
Turn over each piece and cook another 5 minutes.
5
Transfer the cooked tofu to a serving plate.
6
Make¬†seasoning sauce in a small bowl by mixing all ingredients sauce.
7
Spoon the sauce evenly over the tofu, and sprinkle some roasted sesame seeds over top just before serving then serve as a side dish with rice. Happy cooking :-D
